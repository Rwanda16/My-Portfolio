About Me

Hi.

My name is Gustave Habimana and I am a front-end developer.

I was born in a beautiful small town called Kigali in Rwanda, but now reside in Metro-Atlanta Georgia which is a vibrant, fun, and multicultural area with amazing weather.

I am a grizzly alumni with a bachelor's degree in Political Science at Georgia Gwinnett College.

Midway through college, my fascination of making amazing and elegant websites started and from there I learned, and have built beautiful responsive websites such as the ones you're seeing, and I love it.

I love making contend-oriented designs that serve information quick and easy.

Aside from making beautiful websites, I love giving back to the community, whether it be from volunteering my time or talent. I also enjoy playing sports, preferably soccer, and signing in the shower.
